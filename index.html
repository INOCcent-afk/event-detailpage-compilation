    Meet us at Euroshop 2021 in Düsseldorf

    Category / Category

    Date

    March 16, 2021 - 3 days

    Location

    Oxford Falls, NSW, Australia

    Area

    Hall 9 - Stand E73

    Links

    Visit website

    On 16-18 March, we will exhibit at Euroshop in Dusseldorf. 120,000 m2 with 115,000 attendees and 2,500 exhibitors from 60 countries makes the trade fair one of the largest in the world.

    Visit Fagerhult’s stand and experience light. Get to know the latest within lighting, see new products such as the stylish spotlight family and innovative lighting controls. Get inspired with just how lighting can help to create a total retail experience in a shop.

    Monday - Meet Malin Isberg from the company Lexter whom create shop experiences by using sound and scent design.

    Tuesday - Meet Henrik Clausen from Fagerhult Lighting Academy and talk about the importance of light and how light affect us as humans.

    Wednesday - Meet Monica Weinitz the Managing director at Fagerhult Retail AB and talk about our global offering.

    Thursday - Meet Peter Robertson and Simon Wang, the product manager and design engineer manager behind the new Touch spotlight series.

    Get Inspired

    At our stand you can try out different light settings and see what difference light makes. In our Lookbook for lighting you can find the right look for your brand. Get inspired at: fagerhult.com/lookbook.

    Competition

    Each day there is a possibility to win a Audio Pro T3 at our stand. We invite you to enter. The winner will be announced on Fagerhult's Facebook page at the end of each day.

    Contact Aurelie Deliry if you want to book a visit at the stand
